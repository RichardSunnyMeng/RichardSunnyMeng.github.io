About Me

I am pursing my Ph.D. degree at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). Before that, I received my double B.S. degree in Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) in 2020.

My research interests mainly focus on these aspects:
  • AI Safety: Knowledge editing/erasure, truthworthy generation.
  • AI for security: Privacy protection, deepfake detection.
  • AI for science: Medicine, biology.
